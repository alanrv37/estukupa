Marks and Lines (2018)

The following images were created with different mediums to show passage of time considering different life aspects. Each image is 6in x 8in.

The marks throughout this image is indicative of my days working at the animal hospital. The amount of repeated marks signifies the different amount of varieties in each individual day. The boxes are staples learned moments throughout my experiences. Medium: Sharpie

Each stroke of this picture is done by a specific object obtained throughout my life that was important to me. Medium: India Ink

The marks in this image detail different points in time of my life. The thickness and value of the marks indicate an important times in the period of my life. Medium: Graphite

Shapes (2018)

The following image was based on the concept of shapes and how we can use different marks and lines to bring to life an image through the shapes made. The image is 9in x 12in.

The above image is the final rendition considering the composition concept of shapes. The idea for this image is that two contrasting concepts still belong together to create the whole image. Thematically, it was varied sense of direction and it is the connecting factor between the two concepts. The monochrome of the bottom concept compliments the RGB color scale of the top concept to a create a balanced art piece. Mediums: Sharpie, graphite
