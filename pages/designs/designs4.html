DJ Estu Kupa by Alan Vargas Pt. 3

The first rendering of the persona DJ Estu Kupa was created through Adobe Photoshop. The image references the subgenre of EDM called Dubstep. Estu Kupa has a distinct style referencing games and classic video game samples for creating retro sounding beats and drops in combination with the bass heavy and fast beats-per-minute style of music. Estu Kupa’s name derives from Bowser, the Koopa King (Koopa = Kupa).

This final image uses a more modern approach by using different techniques of color application to higlight parts of the image and make it standout against the background. The image reads warm and cool throughout and it keeps a balance between the colors through the method of organizing colors almost at random, but in quadrants. The use of value helps to spotlight certain parts of the image that may have been more dull otherwise, and like the rest of the image, proides a lot of texture to the text.
