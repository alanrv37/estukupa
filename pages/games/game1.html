Freelo Presents: Christmas Crisis

Christmas Crisis was concevied to provide the alternative view to the happy go lucky hoiday season. In this game, the good guys are the bad guys.

It takes an entitle- I mean rightfully deserving young teen to finally take a stand against Santa and his henchmen that won’t release the presents that are rightfully his.

This action packed game provides an alternative approach to the hero/villain relationship and gives a fresh and exciting story filled with many recognizable favorites, such as Frosty the Snowman; Rudolph the rednose Reinder; Elves; and Santa himself.

Background Art

The background art contributed to the final look of the wintery environment. The frozen landscape was done through point lines and curves in illustrator, while the pixelated forest trees were done in Photoshop after a first time introduction to the software in the fall of 2015.
