TFA Presents: Eggman's REvenge

Eggman’s revenge ubilds on the innate response to flea at the site of horror. Dr Eggman proves to be a formidable enemy that has caused a lot of harm to sonic and his friends. It’s up to the player to find their way out of the death trap of a maze that Eggman has created.

The usage of wiring sounds and hard rock give this game the feeling of dread and panic. Through simple player controls the player can navigate around and find areas that can provide a true sense of horror as is the goal of the game.

Genre

3D horror, escape/maze, Strategy

Software

Unity, Photoshop, LogicProX, Audacity

Contributions

Title screen, Intro sequence with music, audio and music integration

Contributions

The color scheme of the title screen are based on key elements, like the big house/mansion that represnts both the vast space of the maze. The high contrast betwen the red and black on the home shows the drastic differences in feeling and imagery that the player would experience in the game.

The most significant contribution was the audio integration and opening sequence music. All of the sounds presnted in a way that best reflected the environment of the maze, but sitll gave sonic the natural feeling of his usual gameplay.
