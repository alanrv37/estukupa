E-Lele

The game E’-Lele centers around the main character E-Lele as they battle their way through the island dangers. E-Lele is on a mission to gather the ancient artifacts to revive the spirit of the island and save his people from devostation.

The main contribution to this game was the design for the title screen, based off an island in Hawaii and the main character E-Lele.
